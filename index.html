Have you ever thought about how much light and waves actually do for us? From the sunlight that wakes you up in the morning to the Wi-Fi that keeps you connected — they're quietly working everywhere, every single day.

On this website, we'll break down how waves and light benefit human life in the most interesting way possible. Easy to read, fun to explore. Let's dive in! 🚀

Devi Salsabila

Hi! I am Devi Salsabila, an undergraduate student majoring in International Program on Science Education UPI. With my strong interest in science, I develop this website to help middle school students understand about The Benefits of Waves and Light for Humans. This website provides students with explanations, activities, and small quiz that support them in building their conceptual understanding. I hope this website can become a helpful learning companion for students and a useful resource for teachers who are interested in innovative science learning. Thank you for visiting, and I hope you enjoy learning science! 🌟
